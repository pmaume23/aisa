Ashinaga Int. Students AssociationTogether we can make a difference

Our association members are part of the Ashinaga Africa Initiative , which consists of scholars and graduates dedicated to bringing a positive change to Africa. AISA members are studying various majors in different universities all across the globe. Our population todate (September 2020), approximates 200 with roughly 4 scholars from each of the 49 sub-Saharan countries in Africa.

Our Mission

To aid individual and collective growth of members through facilitating unity, networking and co-operation within AAI in a development context i.e Sharing information & opportunities, hosting professional development events.

Our Vision

To have scholars cooperate & work together on projects that have an impactful change in AFRICA.
