A I S ATogether we can make a difference

Our association members are part of the Ashinaga Africa Initiative , which consists of scholars and graduates dedicated to bringing a positive change to Africa. AISA members are studying various majors in different universities all across the globe. Our population todate (September 2020), approximates 200 with roughly 4 scholars from each of the 49 sub-Saharan countries in Africa.
